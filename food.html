Famous Dishes

Jowar Bhakri

Traditional flatbread made from sorghum flour served with spicy curries and chutneys.

Shenga Chutney

Dry peanut chutney made with roasted peanuts, garlic, and spices.

Misal Pav

A spicy sprouted lentil curry served with bread.

Puran Poli

Sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and lentils.
